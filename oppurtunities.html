Internship Opportunities

Project Manager Intern

Description: The Intern Project Manager is primarily responsible for managing and supervising the administrative, technical, and financial aspects of given projects from award to completion. The intern will report directly to the Project Manager. The Intern Project Manager will assist with one or more of the PMO's projects, providing an opportunity to gain exposure and understanding of Project Management approaches and processes in a professional environment. Under the guidance of the Project Manager, ensure that awarded projects migrate smoothly from Business Development to the PMO. Assist the Project Manager (PM) in ensuring project requirements, standards, and documentation are met. Assist the project manager in ensuring that projects are on time, within budget, and in scope. Take an active role and exercise critical judgment in developing all project deliverables. Ensure the quality and timely submission of all project deliverables and documentation as allocated by the PM. Assist the PM in reporting on project progress and health. Assist the PM in identifying, managing, and resolving project risks and difficulties. Assist the PM in monitoring quality assurance and change control activities.

Requirements: Proven experience in project management, excellent communication skills, ability to work in a team.

Average Salary: $50,000 per year

Starting Date: May 1, 2024

Project Designer Intern

Description: The Project Design Intern will design advertising materials, annual reports, newsletters, and HTML emails; and manage website presence and functions. Manage the website and Twitter account, make blog posts, and attend various fashion exhibitions and sample salerooms. Create a front-end implementation utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Develop website design skills and study languages like HTML and CSS. Interaction design and data visualization can help to improve and simplify the user interface. Use project management principles to improve process and program effectiveness, reduce costs, and increase efficiency. Review senior students' spring and fall designs. Design intern for a New York-based fashion firm. Defect tracking and retesting are also carried out using QC. Design studies, space diagrams, and construction documentation in REVIT. Implement the design using HTML, JavaScript, JSP, and Java.

Requirements: Proficiency in design software, creative thinking, attention to detail.

Average Salary: $45,000 per year

Starting Date: May 15, 2024
